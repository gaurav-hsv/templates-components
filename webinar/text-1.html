Our diet plays a big role in how we function throughout our day-to-day activities. Today, we are consuming more processed food than ever before due to our busy schedules, easy availability and low prices.

Such processed food makes us feel lethargic all the time. It reduces our energy levels, decreases our overall focus, and inhibits us from functioning at our optimal level. It also makes us restless and irritated with everything around us, further, making it difficult for us to interact with people patiently.

If, after having a good sleep and exercise routine, you still feel tired, irritated and at a low energy level throughout your day, it's probably because of a high consumption of processed food

The science of healthy eating is a live workshop designed to help you achieve maximum energy levels and function at your optimal level everyday by taking up a high nutrient diet.
